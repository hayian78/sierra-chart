## 🏛 Core Market Philosophy

### The Auction is King
Every price move is a search for two-sided trade. I focus on **Value Area (VA)**, **Point of Control (POC)**, and **Excess** to determine if the auction is balanced or trending.

### Cost Basis & Institutional Footprint
I am obsessed with where the "Big Money" is trapped. I monitor **High-Volume Nodes (HVN)** and **Absorption** at key levels to identify institutional cost basis.

### Session Dynamics
I respect the distinct personalities of the **RTH (Regular Trading Hours)** open vs. the **ETH (Electronic Trading Hours)**. I prioritize the NY Open (9:30 AM EST) for volatility and the "afternoon drift" for mean reversion.
